AnalystSequenceFrame

class AnalystSequenceFrame extends Analyst

Analyzes a community using frames containing a constant number of actions. This approach does not necessarily reflect a constant speed of passing of time since moving average time between acts changes as a community grows (more frequent acts) or shrinks (less frequent acts).
